Working with the Port of Newcastle is a dream come true! This multi-faceted business has provided opportunity for us to work on a variety of design and development projects in collaboration with both the Port and eluminate.

As avid Novocastrians we proudly worked on the re-brand of PON which included a wide collection of collateral. Keep your eye out for it around the city.

ADD IN A LINK TO THE WEBSITE AND MAKE ELUMINATE A LINK

This project began with Cordial undertaking an existing collateral audit, assessing marketing elements and utilising all current research that would influence the new brand essence and key messaging. We then began the development of an authentic brand to complement this beautiful region.

Our personal and professional history, along with our local pride motivated us to develop and share this beautiful region with a broader audience. This strategy is to be executed by the with the DRT, Tourism Operators and Visitors.
